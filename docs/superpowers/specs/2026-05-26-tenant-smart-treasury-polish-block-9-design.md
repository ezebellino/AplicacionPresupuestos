# Tenant Smart Treasury Polish Block 9

## Goal

Align `Tesoreria inteligente` with the actual tenant business context.

The current surface is functional, but still carries membership-oriented wording and a few metrics that are not ideal for a company using FacturEasy to manage quotes and collections.

## Scope

Keep the current structure:

- KPI cards
- monthly charts
- insight cards

No new data sources or backend changes are introduced.

## Adjustments

- replace membership-oriented labels with quote and billing language
- keep focus on accepted quotes, pending issued quotes, average ticket, and conversion
- make the insight text more relevant for a small service business

## KPI direction

Recommended cards:

- `Presupuestos aceptados`
- `Facturacion aceptada`
- `Ticket promedio`
- `Conversion`

## Chart direction

Recommended chart wording:

- `Presupuestos aceptados por mes`
- `Meses mas facturados`

## Insight direction

Insights should speak about:

- closure rhythm and accepted volume
- strongest billing month
- pending issued revenue that still requires follow-up

## Out of scope

- new AI features
- predictive analytics
- backend changes
- treasury workflow changes
